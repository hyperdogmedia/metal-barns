Metal Building or Barn? You Decide!

Metal Barns

Perhaps you've thought of building an outbuilding using the same materials that you use in your home for storage of livestock, tools, and equipment. After all, barns are built of wood, aren't they?

Metal structures are increasingly sought-after for garages and barns. They are also used as storage spaces, among other uses. Not only are these structures generally more robust, weather-proof and adaptable than a traditional wooden barn and garage, but they also tend to cost less.

If you've not thought about an iron structure for your barn, now's the time. What you should be aware of regarding metal sheds

They can be as beautiful as a wooden barn

There's no one who wants an unsightly eyesore on their home. The overall look and feel of your home are equally important as its utility for you and your loved ones. If you were thinking that metal sheds would force the user to choose between functionality and form, then think again.

Metal structures can be made to look like traditional barns. Metal barns come with a range of common elements.

metal horse barns with stalls  
  • Gable roofs: Gable roofing are the most prevalent type of barn roof and are prevalent on metal structures. Gable roofs have two sloping sides that meet at an incline at the top. This roof type is great to eject rain and snow however, it can also be utilized to create an internal loft or storage space.
  • Large, open space Barns feature large, open spaces which can be utilized for various purposes. Barns can be utilized for storage of livestock, hay and equipment. Metal buildings are also offered with spacious, open spaces. This makes them perfect for various uses like workshops, garages and space for events.
  • Barns with wide doors typically have wide doors, which allow for easy access to vehicles and equipment. Metal structures can also have doors with a wide width that are ideal for storing huge objects.
  • Metal siding: Metal siding is a common material that is used for the construction of steel buildings. It is strong and easy to keep clean, and can be painted to match the style of a traditional barn.

They can be made to fit your needs for a more barn-like appearance

There are numerous other ways in which a building made of metal could be transformed to look like barns. For example, you can add cupolas, shutters, and other decorative elements to your metal building. You can also paint your metal structure in a barn-style color scheme like red and white.

The choice of a structure made of metal for your barn doesn't have to make you compromise on how you would like your home to look. With their durability and strength as well as their lower cost, and many customizable options to enhance the appearance of a barn-style metal structure could make the best appropriate choice for your requirements and budget.
